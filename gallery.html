Brooklyn Bridge from Manhattan

Brooklyn Bridge is a hybrid cable-stayed/suspension bridge in New York City, spanning the East River between the boroughs of Manhattan and Brooklyn.

Golden Gate Bridge

Golden Gate Bridge is a suspension bridge spanning the Golden Gate, the one-mile-wide (1.6 km) strait connecting San Francisco Bay and the Pacific Ocean.

Skydance Bridge

The Skydance Bridge is a pedestrian bridge and public artwork in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, United States. Skydance bridge was completed 23 April 2012.

Lowry Avenue Bridge

Lowry Avenue Bridge is a steel tied-arch bridge over the Mississippi River in Minneapolis, Minnesota, completed in October 2012.

San Francisco–Oakland Bay Bridge

San Francisco–Oakland Bay Bridge is a complex of bridges spanning San Francisco Bay in California, it carries about 260,000 vehicles a day on its two decks

Arthur Ravenel Jr. Bridge

Arthur Ravenel Jr. Bridge is a cable-stayed bridge over the Cooper River in South Carolina, US, connecting downtown Charleston to Mount Pleasant.

Sunshine Skyway Bridge

Sunshine Skyway Bridge or simply the Skyway, is a cable-stayed bridge spanning the Lower Tampa Bay connecting St. Petersburg, Florida to Terra Ceia.

George Washington Bridge

George Washington Bridge is the world's busiest motor vehicle bridge, carrying over 103 million vehicles in 2016. It is owned by the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey

Mackinac Bridge from the air

Mackinac Bridge is a suspension bridge spanning the Straits of Mackinac to connect the Upper and Lower peninsulas of the U.S. state of Michigan.
